Welcome to MediHelper!

This website is a tool created to help diagnose and treat minor illnesses. Our goal is to reduce waiting times at GPs and pharmacies by helping you get advice from home.

This is NOT an alternative to a professional diagnosis and therefore only addresses minor illness that can be treated with minor medication.

Medication Use Warnings
Paracetamol Mild painkiller, relatively risk-free: Headaches/migraines; toothache;muscle strains;back pains; cold/flu symptoms Do not take with other medication containing paracetamol; using more than reccommended dosage can result in liver failure
Ibuprofen Anti-inflammatory NSAID painkiller: Sore muscles; period cramps; dental pain Talk to a proffessional before taking if you take other medication - anticoagulants, aspirin, blood pressure medication;Can cause stomach ulcers; Do not take if pregnant;
Aspirin Blood thinner: used to reduce inflammation, prevent heart conditions; Talk to a proffessional before taking if you: are pregnant, take other medications such as antidepressants, blood thinners, NSAIDs; can cause allergies; can cause stomach ulcers; increases bleeding/bruising;
